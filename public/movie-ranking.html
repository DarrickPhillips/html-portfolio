The Best Movies by Darrick Phillips

My Top 3 All-Time Favorites

Tommy Boy

Chris Farley was a hilarious actor. David Spade is a great counterpart.
Great Comedy of the 90's.

Black Sheep

Another great comedy by Chris Farley and David Spade.
They were such a comedic duo.

Army of Darkness

Bruce Campbell as Ash in a great Horror mixed with slapstick comedy.
Classic special effects, archaic CGI, and cliche 90's dialogue.
